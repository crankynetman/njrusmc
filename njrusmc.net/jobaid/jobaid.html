Packet Captures

Packet captures help reveal the ground truth. Use these PCAPs as references when troubleshooting complex issues.

Layer-2 and Below

  • Layer-2 Encapsulations Ethernet (with jumbo), PPP, PPPoE, HDLC, ATM-AAL5, and frame-relay captures.
  • Common Non-IP Protocols ARP, CDP, LLDP, VTP, PAgP, LACP (with Cisco Nexus 9K vPC), DTP, and UDLD captures.
  • Legacy Protocols AppleTalk, CLNS, DECnet, DLSw (SNA), and IPX captures.
  • STP Original (PVST), rapid (RPVST), and multiple spanning tree (MST) captures.
  • FHRP HSRP, VRRP, GLBP, IRDP, and IPv6 LAN anycast captures.
  • BFD Mix of single/multi hop, authentication, and echo captures.
  • Wireless LAN Includes many clients, QoS settings, authentication flows, roaming tests, etc.

Tunneling and VPNs

  • IP Tunnels GRE (with options), mGRE/DMVPN, LISP, ERSPAN, IPinIP, AMT, NOS/KA9Q, and RBSCP captures.
  • IPv6 Transition IPv6 in IPv4, GRE, 6to4, 6rd, and ISATAP captures.
  • IPsec and MACsec IPsec: AH/ESP with tunnel/transport mode, GRE, and NAT-T. MACsec: various policies.
  • IP-based L2VPN Many types of L2TPv3, VXLAN, STT, NVGRE, GENEVE, and OTV captures.
  • MPLS LDPv4/v6, pseudowires, RSVP-TE, LSPV (OAM), MPLS over IP/DMVPN, and OSPF/IS-IS TE extensions.
  • Segment Routing SR transmission via OSPF, IS-IS and BGP-LS. Includes SR-TE, SR-OAM, and PCEP. A bit of SRv6.

IP Routing Protocols

  • RIP Versions 1, 2, and ng covering IPv4 and IPv6 exchanges.
  • EIGRP Various EIGRP exchanges (including SIA) for IPv4 and IPv6.
  • OSPF Various OSPFv2 (IPv4) and OSPFv3 (IPv4/IPv6) exchanges.
  • IS-IS Various IS-IS L1 and L2 exchanges for IPv4 and IPv6.
  • BGP For IPv4/v6: unicast, multicast, and flowspec. For MPLS: L3VPN, LDP/BGP VPLS, MVPN, RTC, EVPN, and LS.
  • Babel Various Babel captures (diversity, timestamps, etc.) for IPv4 and IPv6.
  • PIM IPv4/v6 ASM, SSM, Bidir, and BSR. For IPv4: DM, Auto-RP, and PIMv1. For IPv6: Embedded RP.
  • OpenFlow Version 1.3 flow modifications and port statistics with the Faucet SDN controller.

Network Management and Operations

  • Diagnostic TCP/UDP small services (echo, chargen, daytime, etc) and IP SLA probes.
  • QoS All standard DSCP, Ethernet CoS, and MPLS EXP values. ECN, RSVP, and Ethernet pause/PFC frames.
  • NetFlow Versions 5, 9, and 10 (IPFIX) for IPv4, IPv6, and MPLS flows.
  • AAA RADIUS, TACACS, EAP over LAN (802.1X), and TrustSec (SXP) captures.
  • NAT NAT44, NAT64 stateful/stateless, and NAT66 (NPTv6) captures. NAT46 and MAP-E/T coming later.
  • Network Services DHCPv4/v6, DNSv4/v6, PREF64, SNMPv1/v2c/v3, RSH, WOL, RDP, VNC, CMP, syslog, Telnet, and NETBIOS captures.
  • HTTP Versions 1.1, 2, 3 (QUIC), decoded HTTPS, JSON-RPC, and WCCPv2 layer-2/GRE redirection captures.
  • RESTCONF GET, POST, PUT, DELETE, PATCH, and HEAD requests using plain HTTP.
  • gRPC/gNMI Configuration management and telemetry captures. Includes protobuf files for decoding.
  • IoT Protocols Various Internet of Things captures, such as CoAP and MQTT. More coming in the future.
  • Ethernet OAM CFM, E-OAM, and E-LMI, and ITU-T Y.1731 PM captures.
  • ICMP IPv4/v6 captures: ping, traceroute, PMTUD, various unreachables, and IPv6 ND (plus RDNSS).
  • IGMP/MLD IPv4 IGMP and IPv6 MLD captures for ASM/SSM groups, plus DNS and URD-based SSM mapping.
  • IP Extras IPv4 options and IPv6 extension headers, plus IP fragments and UDP-Lite.

Common Applications and Products

  • File Transfer IPv4/v6 FTP, FTPS, SFTP, SCP, and TFTP captures.
  • Email Protocols SMTP, POP3, and IMAP4 captures. Includes relevant mail server DNS A/MX records.
  • Databases MySQL (relational), Mongo (document), and Influx (time-series) captures.
  • Network Storage iSCSI, NFSv3 (with mount and portmap), and SMB captures.
  • Collaboration SIP, SCCP, H.323, MGCP, RTP, DTMF handling, and fax/modem modem captures.
  • Meraki Products Various Cisco Meraki product captures: MR20, MV12WE, and MX64.
